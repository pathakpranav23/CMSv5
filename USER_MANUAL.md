# CMSv5 User Manual

**Welcome to the CMSv5 College Management System!**
This guide helps staff and students navigate the system efficiently.

---

## 1. Getting Started
### Logging In
1.  Open the website URL provided by your administrator.
2.  Enter your **Username** and **Password**.
3.  Click **Login**.
    *   *Note: If you forgot your password, contact the Admin/Clerk.*

---

## 2. For Clerks & Admin (Office Staff)

### A. Managing Students
*   **View Students**: Go to **Students > Student List**. Use filters (Program, Semester) to find specific groups.
*   **Search**: Use the search bar to find students by Name or Enrollment Number.
*   **Edit Profile**: Click the "Edit" button next to a student to update their details (Phone, Address, etc.).

### B. Fees Collection (Quick Pay)
*   **Use this for**: Students paying fees at the counter.
1.  Go to **Fees > Collect Fees (Semester)**.
2.  Select **Program** (e.g., BCA) and **Semester**.
3.  **Search**: Type the student's name in the "Quick Payment" box.
    *   *Tip: The list loads instantly. Just type "Patel" or "1001" to filter.*
4.  Click the student's name to open their Payment Page.
5.  Enter the amount and save/print the receipt.

### C. Subjects & Divisions
*   **Manage Subjects**: Go to **Academics > Subjects** to add or modify course subjects.
*   **Subject Allocation**: Use **Academics > Subject Allocation** to assign elective subjects to students in bulk.

---

## 3. For Faculty Members

### A. Marks Entry
1.  Go to **Exams > Marks Entry**.
2.  Select your **Subject** and **Division**.
3.  Enter marks for each student.
    *   *System Check: The system will warn you if marks exceed the maximum limit.*
4.  Click **Save** often.
5.  Click **Lock** only when you are 100% finished.

---

## 4. For Students

### A. Checking Profile
*   Log in to view your **Dashboard**.
*   Check that your Name, Enrollment Number, and Subjects are correct.

### B. Fees & Results
*   **Fees**: View your paid receipts and pending dues under the **Fees** tab.
*   **Results**: View your internal/external marks and Semester Grade (SGPA) under the **Results** tab.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting (Common Issues)

| Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"Search Unavailable / Server Error"** | Refresh the page. If it persists, ask Admin to "Reload" the server. |
| **Student not found in Search** | Ensure you selected the correct **Program** and **Semester** first. |
| **Cannot Login** | Check Caps Lock. If stuck, ask Admin to reset your password. |
| **Marks not saving** | Check your internet connection. Ensure the exam is not "Locked". |
